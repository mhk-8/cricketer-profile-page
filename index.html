K.L.Rahul

kannur Lokesh Rahul (born 18 april 1992) , commonly known as KL rahul , is an indian international cricketer He generally plays as an opener in Test and T20 game formats and plays in the middle order in the ODIs. Rahul is an occasional wicketkeeper in the shorter formats of the game at the international level
